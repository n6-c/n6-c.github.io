Founder

Anderson Silva

Founder, Node-Six Cybernetics

An engineer and problem-solver who has spent more than a decade across software, artificial intelligence, security, and applied intelligence — and started N6C to put AI to work in the real world.

Anderson founded Node-Six Cybernetics to build the kind of AI he'd spent his career circling: systems that perceive, decide, and act where the stakes are real. He's a software engineer first — more than ten years building systems — with working depth in artificial intelligence and data science.

Running alongside that is a long track in information security and intelligence: years as a security consultant, and time spent teaching strategic intelligence and counterintelligence. It's an unusual pairing, builder and security mind in one, and it happens to be exactly what N6C's work demands, where a system that acts on its own also has to be safe and hard to fool.

He's deliberately broad beyond that, with formal study spanning artificial intelligence, neuroscience, and law. N6C is built in that same image — a company defined by range, not a single lane.

“Professional problem-solver.”
